About Me!

Hey there, It's Hannah Banana, Fee Fi Fo Fana... something like that! Who am I? I am your every day kitty loving, bubbly blonde, explorer, mixed with sugar, spice, and something nice on the rocks.

I grew up in Big Timber Montana. Where the Crazy Mountains are and the friendly people grow. Coming from a small town has taught me two very important lessons. One, If you tell the cop that pulled you over for speeding that your momma is expecting you home in 2 minutes he will send you on your way with a warning, and two, there is no place like Montana.

My hobbies are an array of everything to lounging on my couch with my kitty to cliff jumping hundred foot declines. I am really big in to the outdoors, so much so that my summer job is camping fifty hours a week! Along with being outdoors almost permanently I love photography and have made a small business out of it. I am hoping to make it a career one day.

Dogs or cats? I am definetly more of a dog person but I adopted a ragdoll kitten two years ago and she is the love of my life. I spend a lot of time on my computer due to college and she could not care less. That is eight plus hours a day that she can spend either attacking my toes or snuggling in my blanket.

On a scale of 1- 10 how dorky am I? Well, I have destroyed any evidence of me being a chunky kid, I used to have a lazy eye that miraculously fixed itself, I have Clash Of Clans, and Pokemon Go on my phone, I stutter if I get nervous, and I am currently in Astronomy and am enjoying every bit of it.

My most embarrassing moment? This is probably oversharing.... but I am currently renovating my parents house for cheap rent before they move here in a few years. With all the renovations there are a lot of contractors and electritions running around. Now, for some reason you can hear everything crystal clearly through the vents from one side of the house to the exact opposite. I was awfully sick a few weeks ago and needed to use the ladies room in the worste of ways. I did not think that any of them were at the house yet since it was super early.... Long story short three decently attractive men heard everything.. and I mean everything with crystal clear clarity....I wanted to die of embarrassment when I brought my laundry downstairs about twenty minutes later and saw that they had been down stairs for atleast the last hour...

Finally, do blonde like, really have more fun? Um, totes. I did not pay for a single one of my vodka cranberrys this weekend! And I somehow woke up to a ton of roses in my bed!
